What have we learned at CodeFactory?

At CodeFactory, you receive comprehensive education and training in Git, HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Documentation, and Angular, along with invaluable experience working collaboratively in groups. Upon passing the course, you will possess an impressive portfolio showcasing your excellent talents and knowledge. You will be fully prepared to enter the job market as a skilled mobile, web, and web-app developer.

Real life applications

These skills are directly applicable to real-life job roles, enabling you to develop corporate websites, e-commerce platforms, blogs, and both native and cross-platform mobile apps. By creating responsive and interactive user interfaces, content management systems, and project management tools, you build a robust portfolio that showcases your talents. With hands-on experience in version control using Git, contributing to open-source projects, and writing technical documentation, you are well-prepared to meet the demands of the job market. Your proficiency in agile methodologies and teamwork further enhances your employability, making you a valuable candidate for roles as a mobile, web, and web-app developer.
